The AI Privacy Shield actively monitors all outbound traffic to external AI models. We scan payloads for Personally Identifiable Information (PII) such as Phone numbers, Email addresses, Aadhaar numbers, Passwords, and API Keys.

Any detected sensitive information is automatically redacted before transmission. Files uploaded with direct sensitive content are immediately fully encrypted on local storage using AES-256 for optimal security.
